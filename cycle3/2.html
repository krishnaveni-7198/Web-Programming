ILLIKKAL KALLU

Nestled at a height of 6,000 feet above the sea level in the Western Ghats,Illikkal Kallu is one of the top Kottayam tourist places.

People from all over India come to witness this natural tourist attraction as half of the rock has fallen apart and now what is left is the other half part of it which stands sturdy.

The place is also surrounded by greenery, panoramic vistas, and a utopian atmosphere.

If you are a nature lover and love to explore offbeat places then you must include Illikkal Kallu in your list.
